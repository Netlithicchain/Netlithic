# Netlithic Genesis

Netlithic was created as an open experiment in decentralized infrastructure.

The goal of the project is to explore a blockchain architecture that is
community-driven, transparent, and adaptable to emerging technologies,
including autonomous software agents and AI-native applications.

Rather than launching as a traditional venture-backed blockchain,
Netlithic is designed to grow organically through open participation,
community validation, and public development.

The network is intended to serve as a foundation for builders,
developers, and researchers who want to experiment with decentralized
systems and next-generation digital coordination.

Netlithic begins as a simple protocol.

Its future will be shaped by the community that builds on it.
