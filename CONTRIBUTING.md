# Contributing to Netlithic

We welcome community contributions to the Netlithic protocol.

By submitting code, documentation, or improvements to this repository you agree that:

1. You wrote the contribution or have the right to submit it.
2. Your contribution is licensed under the same MIT license used by Netlithic.
3. The Netlithic project may use, modify, and distribute your contribution.

Please keep pull requests focused and clearly documented.

Thank you for helping build the Netlithic ecosystem.
